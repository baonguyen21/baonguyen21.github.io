About

I am an undergraduate electrical engineering student at Oregon State Unversity. I am interested in digital logic design and device physic and wanted to go on the IC design track at OSU. I am also pursuing a minor in Computer Science and Chemistry.

In the past year, I have been working at the OSU OpenSening Lab where I led a team in hardware design to make a DNA sampling machine from scratch. The job has given me experience in microcontroller, schematic capture, PCB design, and circuit troubleshoot/verification.

I also have experience with microcontroller programming through the home and school projects. Check out my project page for more detail of my work.
